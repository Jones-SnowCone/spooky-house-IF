A Spooky House?

by Joel Whyte

Made using HTML, CSS, and Javascript (with some use of jQuery)

P.S: You can hover certain colored words for more insights into things!

You are inside a relatively spooky house due to a dare you accepted from your friend. You were suppose to stay inside for as long as possible and to explore around the area lest you are called a "chicken".

However, you swore you heard a swish of a door to the left of you...

Then a loud shutter and rattling inside a cage on the floor in front of you, draped with a thick towel...

And finally a squeaky 'erk' of faucets turning from upstairs.

You weigh your options and decide to:

Walk back out of the house and be made fun of as a "coward" (but a smart ones at that). !(Sound Warning)!
Take a peek at the cage and hope the animal inside is being well fed! !(Sound Warning)!
Go upstairs to see what is happening there.
Swing towards the left to see who is paying for the house to be slamming doors around. !(Sound Warning)!
